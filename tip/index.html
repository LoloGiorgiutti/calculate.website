Frequently asked questions
How much should I tip?
Standard tipping in the US is 15–20% for restaurants. 15% is a baseline, 18% is average, 20%+ is for excellent service. For taxis or salons, 10–15% is common.
How is a tip calculated?
Tip = Bill × (Tip% ÷ 100). For example, a 20% tip on $50 = $10 tip, making the total $60. Split 4 ways, each person pays $15.
Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?
Most etiquette guides say to tip on the pre-tax subtotal. However, tipping on the total (including tax) is also common and simpler to calculate.
How do I split the bill with friends?
Enter the total bill, choose your tip %, then press + to add people. The calculator instantly shows how much each person owes including tip.
