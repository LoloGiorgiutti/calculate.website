Hydration tips
  • 1
    Drink a glass of water first thing in the morning — it activates your metabolism and compensates for overnight fluid loss.
  • 2
    Don't wait until you're thirsty. Thirst appears when you're already mildly dehydrated. Drink small amounts regularly throughout the day.
  • 3
    On hot days or during intense exercise, increase your intake by 10–20% and consider electrolytes for sessions over 60 minutes.
  • 4
    Keep a water bottle visible on your desk — it's one of the most effective nudges to drink more throughout the day.
  • 5
    Coffee and tea count toward hydration, but water and herbal teas are most efficient. Sugary drinks are not a good substitute.
