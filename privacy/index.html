Privacy Policy

How we collect and use your information

Last updated: May 2026

1. General information

calculate.website ("the site", "we") is a free online calculator and tools service. This privacy policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

By using this site you agree to this policy. If you disagree, please do not use the service.

2. Information we collect

Data we do NOT collect

calculate.website requires no registration or account. We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or any personally identifiable information directly.

All calculations are performed in your browser (client-side). Values you enter into any calculator are never sent to our servers.

Automatically collected data

Like most websites, our server may automatically log certain standard technical information when you visit:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Traffic source (search, direct, referral)
  • Country and approximate region

This data is used only for aggregate analytics (e.g., understanding which calculators are most popular) and is not linked to any individual user.

3. Cookies

This site uses cookies to improve your experience and to serve advertising. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

First-party cookies

We use technical cookies necessary for basic site functionality, such as storing your theme preference (light/dark mode) and calculator history in your browser's localStorage. These are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

Third-party cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

We may also use Google Analytics or similar services to understand site traffic. These services use their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

4. Advertising

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on users' prior visits to this and other websites.

To opt out of interest-based advertising, visit aboutads.info. For European users, visit youronlinechoices.eu.

5. Data retention

Since we collect minimal data, there is very little to retain. Any anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 26 months. You may request deletion of any data associated with your IP address via the feedback form available on any calculator page.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Since we collect very little personal data, most requests can be fulfilled immediately.

For any privacy-related requests or questions, please use the feedback widget available at the bottom of any calculator page on this site. Select "Suggestion" or describe your request in the message field — we read every submission.

7. Contact

You can reach us through the feedback form embedded at the bottom of each calculator page. There is no need for an email address — simply open any calculator, scroll to the feedback section, and send us your message directly. We review all submissions and respond when a reply is needed.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the last update at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
