How is this calculated?
A deficit of 3,500 kcal burns approximately 1 lb (0.45 kg) of fat. So a daily deficit of 500 kcal creates a weekly deficit of 3,500 kcal = 1 lb/week. Results are estimates — individual metabolism varies.
