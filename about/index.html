About us

Free tools for better everyday decisions

calculate.website is a free collection of simple, accurate calculators for health, finance, math, and everyday life — built for anyone, anywhere in the world. No sign-up, no ads tracking you, no fluff.

Our mission

We believe access to clear, accurate information shouldn't require a subscription, an account, or a degree. Whether you're calculating your BMI, planning a loan, figuring out compound interest, or just counting days until an event — you deserve a fast, honest answer.

Every calculator on this site is built with accuracy and simplicity in mind, with clear explanations of how each result is computed and why it matters.
