Frequently asked questions
How accurate is this clock?
This clock reads the time directly from your device and updates every second. Accuracy depends on your device's system clock, which modern computers synchronize via NTP (Network Time Protocol) to within a few milliseconds.
What is a Unix timestamp?
A Unix timestamp is the number of seconds that have elapsed since January 1, 1970, 00:00:00 UTC. It is the standard way computers represent moments in time internally, independent of timezone or calendar system.
What is ISO 8601 week number?
The ISO 8601 standard defines that week 1 is the week containing the first Thursday of the year. Weeks run Monday–Sunday. This is the most widely used week-numbering system internationally.
How do I use full-screen mode?
Click the expand icon in the top-right corner of the clock. The clock will fill your entire screen — great for presentations, studios or just checking the time from across the room. Click anywhere or press Esc to exit.
